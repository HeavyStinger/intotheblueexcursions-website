Cancellation Policy

As each excursion are tailored made, we kindly ask that you review our cancellation terms below:

To confirm your scenic flight experience, a reservation deposit of $200.00 BZD ($100.00 USD) is required at the time of booking. Any other trips require a deposit of $100.00 BZD ($50.00 USD).

The reservation deposit is non-refundable and secures your scheduled date & time.

Excluding the deposit, cancellations made more than 48 hours prior to departure can receive a refund.

Cancellations made within 48 hours prior to the departure day, are eligible for a 50% refund of the total booking value.

Cancellations made on the same day of departure are non-refundable.

As mentioned each departure is meticulously planned to ensure an exceptional and seamless experience for everyone. Late cancellations impact reserved times and other awaiting guests.

We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise and therefore encourage everyone to notify us at their earliest convenience. We appreciate your time, understanding and cooperation in this matter.
